# quant — 2026-04-11

Version: 1.22.0 -> 1.23.0

The current momentum coefficient of 0.00005 is extremely conservative and likely causing predictions to stick too close to current prices, leading to poor directional accuracy. I'm increasing the momentum coefficient from 0.00005 to 0.0002 (4x increase) to allow momentum signals to have more meaningful impact on predictions. This should help capture actual price movements better while still maintaining reasonable bounds.
