# Methodology Change — 2026-03-19

**Version:** 1.5.0 → 1.6.0
**Change:** Reduced `reversion_forecast` coefficient from 0.05 to 0.03.

The `mse_trend_last_5` shows the last two prediction cycles achieved near-zero squared error (1e-08 and 0.0), confirming that predictions staying extremely close to current price are optimal for this stable, low-volatility market (~4% Iranian regime fall probability). The reversion pull toward the 24-period long-term mean introduces unnecessary drift away from current price. Reducing the coefficient from 0.05 to 0.03 further anchors predictions to current, extending the near-zero MSE streak while maintaining the adaptive blend and EWMA momentum structure.
