# quant — 2026-03-29

Version: 1.7.0 -> 1.8.0

The current momentum coefficient of 0.02 * horizon_scale is still causing predictions to deviate too far from current prices, leading to high MSE. I'm reducing the momentum coefficient from 0.02 to 0.005, making it 4x smaller. This should keep predictions much closer to current prices and reduce the massive MSE penalty. The recent MSE trend shows improvement with smaller coefficients, so further reduction should help. This change maintains the adaptive blending and volatility-based adjustments while making the overall model much more conservative.
