# Methodology Change — 2026-03-17

**Version:** 1.3.0 → 1.4.0

**Change:** Set `NEWS_FEATURE_WEIGHT = 0.0` (was 0.01) to disable news-based forecast adjustment entirely.

**Rationale:** With 0% directional accuracy across all 6 predictions and MSE 136× worse than naive baseline, the agent was systematically wrong on direction every cycle. The `effective_news_weight` formula (`NEWS_FEATURE_WEIGHT / max(vol/median_vol, 0.5)`) amplifies news weight when volatility is low relative to its median — which is the normal regime for this market. Iranian regime-fall news is escalation-skewed by nature, so this consistently pushed predictions upward while the market price stayed flat or drifted down. Zeroing the news weight lets the EWMA + mean-reversion statistical core operate cleanly without systematic directional bias from news sentiment.
