# Quant Methodology Change — 2026-03-18

**Version:** 1.4.0 → 1.5.0

**Change:** Reduced `momentum_forecast` pull coefficient from `0.15` to `0.08`.

**Reasoning:** Overall MSE is 0.00507923 — 40× worse than the naive baseline — driven largely by early cycles with large errors (~10–12pp). The MSE trend last 5 cycles is `[0.00971611, 0.01317766, 7e-07, 0.00049948, 3.15e-05]`, showing clear recent improvement. The primary risk factor is the EWMA lag: when the market moves quickly, `ewma - current` can be large, and the 0.15 coefficient amplifies that into a noticeable deviation from current price. Halving the coefficient to 0.08 further anchors the forecast to current price, limiting the impact of EWMA lag in the rare cases where the market makes a sudden move. Per-market directional accuracy is already 91.67%, so the problem is purely magnitude — smaller corrections reduce squared error in a stable low-vol regime.
