# Historian Methodology Change — 2026-03-17

**Version:** 1.3.0 → 1.4.0
**Change:** Reduced `MAX_NEWS_SHIFT` from 0.025 to 0.010

## Reasoning

Historian overall MSE (9.109e-05) remains 1.6× the naive baseline (5.714e-05). The most recent cycle produced the worst MSE of the last five (0.00018522), consistent with a pattern of alternating good/bad cycles driven by the news signal. The naive baseline MSE implies ~0.76pp average 4-hour price movement; `MAX_NEWS_SHIFT = 0.025` (2.5pp) is roughly 3× that magnitude, meaning the news component can easily dominate and push the forecast well beyond what actually materializes in any given window. This continues the tightening trend from 1.2.0 (0.05 → 0.025): reducing to 0.010 caps the news contribution to ~1pp, closer to the scale of real 4-hour moves, while preserving the directional signal. Reversion was already tightened to 0.01 in 1.3.0; the news cap is now the logical next constraint.
