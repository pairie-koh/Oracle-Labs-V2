# Methodology Change — 2026-03-19

**Version:** 1.5.0 → 1.6.0
**Change:** Increased `MAX_NEWS_SHIFT` from 0.010 to 0.015

## Reasoning

The scorecard shows 94.44% directional accuracy on regime_fall — we are calling the direction correctly almost every time. However, overall MSE (0.00011598) remains above the naive baseline (0.00009999), meaning our magnitude of shift is sometimes insufficient to capture actual price moves.

With the current 1pp cap, any genuine market-moving news that causes a price shift greater than 1pp leaves us under-corrected relative to where the price actually lands. Since our directional signal is highly reliable, widening the cap to 1.5pp should reduce MSE on cycles where actual price movement exceeds the old cap.

The last two cycles were excellent (MSE 1.72e-06 and 5.76e-06), confirming the current framework is sound. This is a conservative, directional expansion — not a structural change — intended to close the remaining gap to the naive baseline.
