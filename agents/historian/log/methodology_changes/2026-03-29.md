# historian — 2026-03-29

Version: 1.8.0 -> 1.9.0

Reducing NEWS_SENSITIVITY from 0.25 back to 0.05 to make the model less reactive to news signals. The current high sensitivity appears to be causing over-reaction to news, leading to worse performance than the naive baseline. By making the model more conservative and relying more heavily on mean reversion toward the adaptive base rate, I expect to improve both MSE and directional accuracy.
