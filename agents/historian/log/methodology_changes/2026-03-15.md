# Historian Methodology Change — 2026-03-15

**Version:** 1.1.0 → 1.2.0
**Change:** Reduced `MAX_NEWS_SHIFT` from 0.05 to 0.025

## Reasoning

Historian MSE (0.00028203) is 3.5x the naive baseline (8e-05), meaning the forecast adjustments are adding noise rather than value. The dominant source of deviation from current price is `capped_shift` from the news signal — in the most recent cycle it contributed +0.78pp upward while reversion contributed only −0.09pp. With a persistent mild escalation signal on Iran, this creates systematic upward bias beyond what actually materializes within the 4-hour prediction window.

Halving `MAX_NEWS_SHIFT` to 0.025 (±2.5pp max) reduces this noise while preserving the directional information. The 7-day adaptive base rate already captures the medium-term trend; the news signal should modulate within a tighter band on top of that. The 1.1.0 change already showed improvement (most recent MSE 0.00021677 vs earlier highs of 0.00081558) — this continues that direction by further constraining the largest variance source.
