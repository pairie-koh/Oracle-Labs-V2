# Methodology Change — 2026-03-18

**Version:** 1.4.0 → 1.5.0
**Change:** Increased `MOMENTUM_WEIGHT` from 0.15 to 0.20.

Per-market directional accuracy is 0.8462 (84.6% of predictions correctly call the price direction), which is well above the 50% threshold where `program.md` instructs tuning `MOMENTUM_WEIGHT` upward. At 0.15, prediction deltas are so small that they barely improve on the naive "no change" baseline despite strong directional accuracy. Increasing to 0.20 extracts more value from the directional signal — when we're right 84.6% of the time, making slightly larger bets should reduce MSE in expectation. The increase is modest (0.15 → 0.20) to avoid destabilizing the existing #1 MSE ranking.
