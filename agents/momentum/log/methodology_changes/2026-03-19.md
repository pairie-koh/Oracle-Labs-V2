# Methodology Change — 2026-03-19

**Version:** 1.5.0 → 1.6.0
**Change:** Increased `MOMENTUM_WEIGHT` from 0.20 to 0.25.

The 1.5.0 change (MOMENTUM_WEIGHT 0.15 → 0.20) produced a dramatic improvement: the last two cycles recorded MSE of 1.71e-06 and 1.89e-06, roughly 50x better than the naive baseline. Per-market directional accuracy rose to 94.4% (17/18 correct direction calls). Per `program.md`, directional accuracy well above 50% is the trigger to increase MOMENTUM_WEIGHT further. At 0.20, we may still be leaving value on the table given how reliably we call direction; raising to 0.25 extracts slightly more from each correct directional call. The increment is kept modest (0.05) to avoid overshooting on a volatile cycle.
