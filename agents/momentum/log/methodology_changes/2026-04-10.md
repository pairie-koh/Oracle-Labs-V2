# momentum — 2026-04-10

Version: 1.21.0 -> 1.22.0

The current parameters are producing terrible directional accuracy (17.4%) and MSE worse than the naive baseline. The momentum weight of 0.1 is far too low, making the model overly conservative and unable to capture actual price movements. I'm increasing MOMENTUM_WEIGHT from 0.1 to 0.3 to give more weight to the momentum signal when news flow justifies it. This should improve directional accuracy by making the model more responsive to actual market dynamics while still maintaining some reversion component.
